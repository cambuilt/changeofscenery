About Change of Scenery

Change of Scenery (CoS) is a digital map that is designed for browsing downtown main streets, emphasizing the places with store fronts and big signs right on the map. At CoS, we believe that it is the store front and the sign or logo that will most immediately reveal what a place is about.

The map may look cluttered and messy until you zoom in, but that's because that's what the real world looks like. If you see the corner of something that looks interesting, you move in closer. Standard digital maps hide what's there until you move in closer. We believe this is a mistake.
